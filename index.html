MEDDIC in Practice
M

Metrics

Every discovery note contains a measurable business outcome. Quantify the pain before booking the meeting.

E

Economic Buyer

Every meeting booked includes EB identification. Who controls the budget? Not just the friendliest contact.

D

Decision Criteria

Gift to the AE — map the evaluation criteria before the first AE call. Don't start from zero.

D

Decision Process

Timeline, stakeholders, approval steps. Map the buying committee, not just one contact.

I

Identify Pain

Specific, urgent pain — not "interested in learning more." BVS template ensures this is in every note.

C

Champion

Tested, not assumed. Who will sell internally for us? Identify and verify before handoff.
