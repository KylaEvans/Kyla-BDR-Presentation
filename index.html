Coaching Philosophy

3 Anchors:

We do incredible product training at Salesforce. What hasn't always matched that investment is development of sales skills themselves — cold call craft, discovery excellence, objection handling, executive presence. A BDR can know every Salesforce product and still struggle on the phone. That gap is what my coaching philosophy is designed to close.

Right now, BDRs are generating Stage 2s for the optics — to hit the KPI. Quality pipeline is the #1 concern from AE and leadership. My coaching approach directly addresses this: I coach to what makes a meeting worth booking, not how many meetings to book. Quality discovery notes, MEDDIC-aligned qualification, AE-ready handoffs. That's what builds trust with AEs and converts pipeline to ACV.

MEDDIC in Practice
M

Metrics

Every discovery note contains a measurable business outcome. Quantify the pain before booking the meeting.

E

Economic Buyer

Every meeting booked includes EB identification. Who controls the budget? Not just the friendliest contact.

D

Decision Criteria

Gift to the AE — map the evaluation criteria before the first AE call. Don't start from zero.

D

Decision Process

Timeline, stakeholders, approval steps. Map the buying committee, not just one contact.

I

Identify Pain

Specific, urgent pain — not "interested in learning more." BVS template ensures this is in every note.

C

Champion

Tested, not assumed. Who will sell internally for us? Identify and verify before handoff.
